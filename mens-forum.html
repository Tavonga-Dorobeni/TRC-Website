Angels' Ministry

Summary

Jesus said, “Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.” (Mt 19 vs14 NIV). And only when he was done blessing them did he leave.

Angel’s Ministry, as our Sunday school is known as, is headed by dedicated teachers who understand that the children are the future. A strong tree begins with a good seed and so the Angel’s Ministry, with its congregation of bright children ranging from as young as two years to twelve years, are ensuring a future of exceptional ambassadors for Christ. Every Sunday these young boys and girls learn the importance of praising and honoring our mighty God through worship and memorizing bible verses. Because Throne Room Ministries realizes that knowledge of the bible builds an obedient and God-fearing individual, an big investment of time and patience has been put for the children through a minimum four teachers at a time.
